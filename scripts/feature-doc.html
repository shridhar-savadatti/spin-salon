Section 01

Customer-Facing Features

What your customers see and interact with on your website.

📅

Online Appointment Booking

Customers book 24/7 from any device — no calls needed. Choose service, stylist, date and time in under 2 minutes.

  • Multi-service booking in one go
  • Quantity selector (e.g. 2× Haircut)
  • Stylist preference selection
  • GST-inclusive pricing shown upfront
  • Promo code / discount support
  • WhatsApp confirmation on booking
📢

Live Announcement Bar

A scrolling banner at the top of the site showing your current offers — updated from the admin panel instantly.

  • Multiple offers scroll automatically
  • Color-coded per offer
  • Clicking takes customer to booking
  • Admin can add/remove any time
✨

Hero Offer Badge

Your top offer is highlighted as a glowing badge on the homepage hero — front and center for every visitor.

  • Links directly to booking with service pre-selected
  • Color matches the announcement bar
  • Zero-click path to conversion
💬

WhatsApp & Call CTAs

Book, Call, and WhatsApp buttons prominently placed for customers who prefer direct contact.

  • Pre-filled WhatsApp messages
  • Click tracking via analytics
  • Works on mobile natively
🗺️

Location & SEO

Your salon shows up in "unisex salon near me" searches with structured data, FAQs, and neighbourhood coverage built in.

  • Google Business Schema markup
  • FAQ section for local search
  • Neighbourhood chips (HSR, Silk Board…)
  • Sitemap + robots.txt
⭐

Testimonials & Gallery

Showcase customer reviews and a photo gallery to build trust and drive conversions.

  • Star ratings displayed
  • Mobile-optimised gallery
  • Services highlight section
Section 02

Admin Dashboard

Everything you need to run the salon — from one screen, on any device.

🔔

Real-Time Booking Alerts

Never miss a booking. The admin panel sounds a loud alarm and flashes a banner the moment a new booking comes in.

  • Loud repeating alarm until dismissed
  • Push notifications on phone
  • App icon badge count
  • Auto-polls every 30 seconds
🧾

Billing POS

A full point-of-sale terminal. Add services, search customer, apply discount, charge — and print or WhatsApp the invoice.

  • Service grid with category tabs
  • Customer search with visit history
  • % or flat discount
  • Cash / UPI / Card payment
  • GST split (CGST + SGST)
  • Professional invoice with print/PDF
  • WhatsApp invoice to customer
📋

Appointments Management

View, confirm, complete or cancel all bookings. Filter by status, stylist, date or search by name.

  • Walk-in booking from admin
  • Stylist assignment
  • Status workflow: Pending → Confirmed → Completed
  • WhatsApp notify on status change
  • Past & upcoming grouped by date
👥

Customer Directory

All your customers in one place — from bookings and imports. See visit count, last visit, and message them on WhatsApp.

  • Search by name or phone
  • Visit count & last visit date
  • One-tap WhatsApp button
  • Import from Excel
  • Auto-populated from bookings
📣

Campaign Messaging

Send personalised WhatsApp messages to customers in bulk — target by last visit date or service type.

  • Custom message templates
  • Filter: "visited 4+ weeks ago"
  • Filter: by service category
  • Track sent / total count
🏷️

Promo Offer Codes

Create and manage discount codes for new or returning customers — percentage or fixed amount.

  • Code: WELCOME20, FLAT200, etc.
  • Set expiry date & max uses
  • New client / all client targeting
  • Usage count tracking
  • Enable / disable instantly
